Building a healthy plate!

A healthy meal matters!!

A healthy plate gives our body the energy required to play, study, think and grow properly.It helps our body function without any fault.

Smart Snack Choises!!

Snacks are better when they give us energy and nutrition instead of health problems.Fruits, nuts, yoghurt and other healthy homemade foods are better than chips, sugary drinks, and biscuits.

Explore more nutrition facts at: WHO Healthy Diet Guide

Nutrition tips for all!

1.Drink enough water!!

Water helps the body stay active, supports digestion, and keeps the brain focused.Everyone should preferebally carry a water bottle where ever they go.

2.Eat colorful food!!

Different colors in a meal includes different fruits and vegetables. It often means more nutrition and a good meal.
